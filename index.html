We're coming for the wedding, what is there to do here?

We are getting married in Clintonville (north Columbus) but Short North is very nearby, you can check out The Ohio State University (where Meris works), the capital (where Derek spends some weekdays), German Village, the Brewery District and so much more!

There is COSI (children's science and industry museum), the Columbus Zoo, or the Columbus Museum of Art! Links to all of these and more are available at the Visitor's Bureau

Some of our favorite restaurants and eateries are:

  • The Old Mohawk (German Village)
  • Local Ice Cream (Jeni's or Graeter's)
  • The Pearl (Short North)
  • Natalie's Coal Fire Pizza (Clintonville)
